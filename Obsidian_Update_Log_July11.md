# July 11, 2026 - TLCS Update Log

## Divergence Exits Update

**Pine Script (Elite Indicator)**
- Added logic to exit trades based on divergence signals (`D` and `H` markers) generated by the TLCS AI Oscillator.
- Mapped the external divergence signal array via `input.source()` for isolated indicator communication.
- Added a `divExitTriggered` boolean to the `TradeLogic` type.
- Updated `evaluateTradeProgress` to set `trade.divExitTriggered = true` and `trade.isClosed = true` when an active trade encounters a divergence matching its direction (Red for Long, Green for Short).
- Updated `finalizeTrade` to dynamically assign `_outcome := 'Divergence Exit'` to differentiate it from standard EOD or Invalidation "Force Closes".

**Frontend (Web & Mobile Apps)**
- Since divergence closures do not contain a fixed technical level (like TP or SL), the `exact_pct` payload handles strict mathematical resolution of the trade as `WIN` or `LOSS`.
- Updated `getExitLevel` in `trade-metrics.js` and `page.tsx` to trap `st.includes('DIVERGENCE')` and return a new `DIV` category.
- Created a standalone `DIV` outcome filter tab in `metrics.html` and `page.tsx` for tracking divergence-based trade exits specifically in the 'Today's Market Guidance' section.
